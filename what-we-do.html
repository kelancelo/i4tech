What we do

What we do

We are a leading Systems Integrator in the Industry 4.0 space.

Our mission is helping customers achieve their business objectives by translating data into actionable intelligence. We help manufacturing companies optimize operations, increase productivity, control product quality, enhance supply chain agility and improve time to market, amongst other challenges that can be addressed with the adoption of data-driven operations.   With a focus in South Asia and presence in Singapore, Thailand, India and Vietnam, i4TECH is composed of subject matter experts coming from leading industry players to bring a pragmatic, customer-centric approach to the local industries.

Our Expertise

Our teams architect and deploy digital manufacturing solutions for Consumer Package Goods, Automotive and process Industries.

With a diverse background in Automation, Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES), Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), Cloud and Data Analytics, we adopt an agile approach in the digitalization journey with our customers.

Technologies we use

The most powerful combination of traditional automation and the latest technologies for Industry.

While we have deep expertise in the traditional Automation and MES platforms and practices, at i4TECH we leverage the latest technologies such as Industrial IoT and SCADA platforms, low-code development environments, cloud services and open-source tools. We recommend avoiding monolithic implementation in benefit of scalable, rapid development of business-oriented solutions for quick ROI and agile adaptability to changing customer’s needs.
